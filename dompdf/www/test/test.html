Investment Development Team

The city council’s Investment Development Team can help you if you're looking to relocate your business to Stoke-on-Trent or need to expand your existing operation within the city.

Specifically we can help with:

  • information to help you to choose the optimum location
  • identification of suitable sites and premises
  • advice on available grants and incentives
  • support in relation to skills and training
  • help with logistics such as site visits and area tours
  • assistance with industry and market research
